# Client Intake Form

Date: 2026-05-17

Use this form for non-designers. Keep it simple. The goal is to collect enough signal to create a premium direction without overwhelming the client.

## Basic Info

1. Business or project name:
2. Website URL, if any:
3. Main contact:
4. Deadline or launch window:

## Offer

1. What do you sell or offer?
2. What is the main thing you want people to understand?
3. What should visitors do? Choose one primary action:
   - Book a call
   - Buy now
   - Join waitlist
   - Request quote
   - Download
   - Contact
   - Learn more
   - Other:

## Audience

1. Who is the website for?
2. What do they care about most?
3. What objections or doubts do they have?
4. What should they believe after 10 seconds?

## Desired Feeling

Choose up to three:

- Premium
- Calm
- Technical
- Luxurious
- Fast
- Trustworthy
- Playful
- Editorial
- Cinematic
- Minimal
- Bold
- Warm
- Exclusive
- Intelligent
- Human
- Other:

## Source Materials

1. Links you like:
2. What do you like about them?
3. Links you dislike:
4. What should we avoid?
5. Upload or link images, screenshots, moodboards, logos, product shots, or videos:
6. Do you own these assets or are they inspiration only?

## Brand and Content

1. Do you have a logo?
2. Do you have brand colors or fonts?
3. Do you have existing copy?
4. Do you have testimonials, metrics, case studies, reviews, or proof?
5. Do you have product screenshots or demos?

## Practical Constraints

1. How many pages are needed?
2. Is ecommerce needed?
3. Are forms, booking, login, dashboard, or CMS needed?
4. Any legal/compliance requirements?
5. Any must-use tools, platforms, or hosting?

## Permission Check

For every link or asset:

- Owned by us
- Owned by client
- Licensed
- Inspiration only
- Unknown

If unknown, treat as inspiration only.

## Final Question

If this website succeeds, what changes for the business?
